Key Limitations vs Google Translate

DeepL does not support Hindi, Bengali, Swahili, Tagalog, Persian, Hebrew, Thai, Vietnamese, Malay, or most African languages. If your translation workflow includes these languages, Google Translate or Microsoft Translator are required for those specific pairs.

DeepL also lacks real-time camera translation (Google Translate's camera feature) and doesn't have a standalone mobile app that works offline (Google Translate supports offline download packs).

Language added recently: Arabic was added in 2023, Korean in 2022. DeepL has been slowly expanding its language list. Check deepl.com/languages for the most current list, as new languages are added periodically.
