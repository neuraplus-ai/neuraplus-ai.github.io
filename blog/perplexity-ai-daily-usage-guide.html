Perplexity AI Daily Usage Guide: How to Use It Every Day in 2026

PL
Prashant Lalwani2026-04-21 · 14 min read
Perplexity AIAI Search

Perplexity AI is most valuable when it becomes a daily habit rather than an occasional tool. Here is a practical guide to integrating it into your work and personal life every day.

Try it free: Perplexity AI is available at perplexity.ai with a generous free tier. Pro plan ($20/month) unlocks unlimited Pro Search, GPT-4o and Claude models, and file upload analysis.

Morning: 5-Minute Intelligence Brief

Start each day with two queries:

  1. "Top 5 news stories in [your industry] from the last 24 hours" — gets you briefed without opening 10 sites
  2. "Any significant developments in [specific topic you track] this week?" — keeps you updated on your niche

Use News Focus mode for both. This replaces a 20-minute news reading session with a 3-minute brief.

During Work: Replace Google for Research Queries

The habit shift that saves the most time: every time you would open Google to research something (not navigate somewhere), use Perplexity instead. Examples:

  • Looking up a stat for a report → Perplexity with Academic Focus
  • Checking a competitor's recent activity → Perplexity with News Focus
  • Understanding a technical concept → Perplexity with Web Focus
  • Finding community opinions → Perplexity with Reddit Focus

Writing: Use Perplexity for Research, Not Writing

Perplexity is a research tool, not a writing tool. The optimal workflow: use Perplexity to gather facts, statistics, and perspectives with citations → use those citable facts in your writing (Claude or ChatGPT for the actual drafting). This produces more accurate, better-cited content than using an AI writing tool alone.

Keyboard Shortcuts to Know

  • Ctrl/Cmd + K — New search from anywhere
  • Tab — Cycle through Focus modes
  • Ctrl/Cmd + Enter — Submit with Pro Search
  • Click numbered citation — Opens source in sidebar

Building the Habit

The fastest way to build the Perplexity habit: set it as your browser's default search engine. When you type in the address bar, your query goes to Perplexity. After one week, you will naturally learn which query types work best and route accordingly. Most users report it feels completely natural within 5–7 days.

Setting Perplexity as default search: Go to perplexity.ai → Settings → Set as default search engine. Follow your browser's prompts. Takes 30 seconds.
