The "Secret Weapon" Workflow

If you're a solo creator or a freelancer managing multiple clients, here is the exact stack I recommend to scale your production without losing your mind:

  1. Ideate & Script: Use Claude 3.5 to outline the video and write a killer hook. Feed it your past scripts so it matches your voice.
  2. Record: Hit record. Don't worry about mistakes; just keep talking.
  3. Rough Cut: Drop the footage into Descript. Let the AI remove all the "ums," "uhs," and dead silence in one click.
  4. Repurpose: Take the final long-form video, drop it into Opus Clip, and let it generate 10 Shorts with auto-captions.
  5. Package: Generate 3 background concepts in Midjourney, bring them into Canva, add your bold text, and A/B test them.
