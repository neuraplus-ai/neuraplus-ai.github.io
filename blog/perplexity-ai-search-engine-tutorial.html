Perplexity AI Search Engine Tutorial: Step-by-Step 2026

PL
Prashant Lalwani2026-04-10 · NeuraPulse
15 min readTutorialSearch Engine

Perplexity AI looks simple — just a search bar. But beneath that simplicity is a powerful set of features that most users never discover. This complete tutorial walks through every feature systematically, from your first search to advanced Pro techniques, with real examples you can try immediately.

💡 Before You Start: Go to perplexity.ai right now and keep it open alongside this guide. The best way to learn Perplexity is by doing. No account needed to start.

Understanding the Perplexity Interface

When you first open Perplexity, you see a clean interface with:

  • Search bar (center): Where you type questions — full sentences work best
  • Focus selector (left of search): The circle icon — click to choose All, Academic, Writing, YouTube, Reddit, or Math
  • Attach button: Upload files (Pro only) for document analysis
  • Pro Search toggle: Available next to submit — activates 5-step deep search
  • Left sidebar: Your search history and saved Spaces
  • Discover tab: Curated trending topics and popular searches

Basic Search Mastery

Writing Better Queries

Perplexity understands natural language — full questions outperform keyword strings:

Weak Query (keyword)Strong Query (natural language)
climate change 2026What are the most significant climate change developments in 2026 and what policies have been implemented?
python vs javascriptWhen should I choose Python over JavaScript for a new project in 2026 and what are the main trade-offs?
inflation IndiaWhat is the current inflation rate in India in 2026, what is causing it, and how is the RBI responding?

Reading Your Results

After a search, you see: the synthesized answer at top, numbered source citations in brackets throughout the text, a sources sidebar on the right showing all cited URLs, suggested follow-up questions below the answer, and sharing/copy options. The most important habit: click the citation numbers for any fact you plan to use or that seems surprising. This verifies accuracy and gives you the primary source for further reading.

Focus Modes — Your Secret Weapon

Focus modes restrict which sources Perplexity searches — dramatically improving result quality for specific use cases:

🌐

All (Default)

Searches the entire web. Best for general queries, current events, and everyday research. Use this 80% of the time.

🎓

Academic

Prioritizes journals, .edu sites, research papers, and scholarly publications. Use for thesis research, medical queries, and any topic requiring peer-reviewed sources.

✍️

Writing

Optimized for content creation, grammar help, and writing assistance. Surfaces style guides, writing tips, and editorial resources.

🧮

Math

Activates computational mode with step-by-step equation solving. Type equations directly or describe the math problem in words.

Pro Search — Step-by-Step Walkthrough

To use Pro Search: before clicking submit, toggle the "Pro" button next to the search bar (or press Shift+Enter). You'll see Perplexity pause and sometimes ask a clarifying question — answer it for best results.

Example: Type "What are the best no-code AI tools for building business apps in 2026?" with Pro Search ON. Perplexity might ask: "Are you looking for tools for internal business apps or customer-facing products?" — answering this guides the 5-step search to more relevant results.

When to Use Pro vs Standard Search

  • Use Standard: Quick factual lookups, current news, simple "what is X" questions, searches where you need fast results
  • Use Pro Search: Complex multi-part questions, research requiring multiple perspectives, technical deep-dives, when standard results feel superficial

Spaces and Pages — Power User Features

Creating a Space

Click "Spaces" in the left sidebar → "New Space." Give it a name (e.g., "Thesis Research — AI Ethics"). Set custom instructions for how the AI should behave in this space — "Always cite peer-reviewed sources, focus on developments from 2023-2026, use formal academic language." All searches within this Space inherit these instructions.

Creating a Page

After any research thread you're proud of, click the Share button and select "Create a Page." Perplexity formats the conversation into a document with proper headings, preserved citations, and a clean layout. You get a shareable URL immediately. Great for: sending research to a professor, sharing findings with teammates, creating a personal reference document.

Tips, Tricks and Shortcuts

  • Shift + Enter: Activate Pro Search on any query
  • Type "ELI5:" before any question to get a simplified, jargon-free explanation
  • Type "Academic focus:" inline if you forget to select the mode
  • Use "Compare X and Y" questions — Perplexity excels at structured comparisons
  • Star important answers to save them to your history for later reference
  • Share a thread URL from any conversation for quick sharing with collaborators
  • Mobile app: Available for iOS and Android — same features, works offline for saved content

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get better results from Perplexity AI?+

Five practices for better results: (1) Write full questions, not keywords. (2) Switch to Academic Focus for research requiring peer-reviewed sources. (3) Use Pro Search for complex multi-part questions. (4) Ask follow-up questions to drill deeper rather than starting a new search. (5) Click citation numbers to verify key facts and discover primary sources for further reading. These five habits dramatically improve the quality and usefulness of Perplexity outputs.

Q: Is there a Perplexity AI app?+

Yes — Perplexity AI has mobile apps for both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). The apps include all core features: search, Pro Search, Spaces, Pages, and voice input. The mobile app supports voice queries, which is particularly useful for quick research questions on the go. All Pro features are available on mobile with a Pro subscription.

Q: Can I use Perplexity AI in India?+

Yes — Perplexity AI is fully available in India with no restrictions. The service works in all major Indian cities and is accessible through standard internet connections. Payment for Pro ($20/month) can be made with Indian credit/debit cards. The service searches Indian news sources, government websites, and regional content alongside international sources, making it well-suited for India-specific research queries.
