Hidden Costs to Watch

  • Egress fees: All providers charge for data egress. CoreWeave charges $0.10/GB, comparable to AWS ($0.09/GB) and GCP ($0.08/GB). For model serving with large outputs, these add up.
  • Storage: CoreWeave charges $0.039/GB/month for SSD-backed storage — competitive with AWS EBS ($0.08/GB).
  • Committed use discounts: CoreWeave offers significant discounts (20–40%) for 6–12 month commitments. If your GPU usage is predictable, committed pricing dramatically reduces costs.

Cost Optimisation Strategies on CoreWeave

  1. Scale to zero — CoreWeave charges by the second. Shut down idle inference servers overnight and weekends (save 50-65% on non-production workloads).
  2. Mix instance types — Use L40S ($1.14/hr) for inference and H100 SXM ($2.79/hr) only for training. Many models serve well on L40S.
  3. Spot instances — CoreWeave offers spot pricing at 30–60% discount for interruptible training jobs.
  4. Committed use — Sign a 6-month agreement for your baseline capacity and pay spot/on-demand only for overflow.

Bottom line: CoreWeave saves AI companies 20–35% on GPU costs versus AWS and Google Cloud on equivalent hardware. For startups running $50,000+/month in GPU spend, this represents $10,000–$17,500 in monthly savings — meaningful capital that extends runway significantly.
